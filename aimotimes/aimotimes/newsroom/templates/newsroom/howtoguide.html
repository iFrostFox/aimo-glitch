How to write&publish newspapers

Author: @ArcticFox

1- Go to admin page(/admin)

2- Log in with your account

3- Press newspapers

4- Create a newspaper

5- IMPORTANT NOTE: Slug field determines the url so don't make is something weird, if you write "aaa" at the slug field the newspaper will be viewed from /newsroom/newspaper/aaa

How to Insert Images:

Upload image to Imgur, right-click(PC/Mac) or long-press(Phones/Tablets) then select "Open Image in New Tab" and copy the URL

When you're writing the newspaper click the image icon and paste the URL, do not write anything at the other parts, it will seem at it's original size
